About Us

Our Journey started with our pig named Aero, a small sawmill to build our home & a garden that continues to get bigger every year.  We are a family of three that is constantly evolving and growing. Our mission is to show others organic gardening is the way to go & that you can build up any soil, even sand, to create your own magical paradise.

The begining of our home

The process of building our home was exciting and hard, but we knew it would be worth it. We always wanted our home to be free from chemicals & plywood, so that is why we started from scratch, one log at a time.

Our 1st Sawmill 

A log master LM1, gas powered, hand crank, & push feed. When we picked it up from the train station we were so excited!  Once we got it home, we didn't know how we would set it up, after all it weighed over 1400 lbs & we didn't have our loader yet. So we got creative, and used a pulley, some chains and a tree. After everything was set up the milling began, & the hard work has never ended. Our only regret was not getting a power feed system sooner. 

The begining of our gardens

We started our gardens before we started anything, we didnt even have fences till later on. Right away we started planting fruit trees, blueberries, hardy kiwis & more & we've never stopped. Every year we order at least 100 trees, this year it was 100 apricot trees. Our place becomes more & more of a jungle every year & we can't wait to plant more!

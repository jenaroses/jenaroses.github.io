BEYOND ORGANIC
GARDENS

We grow a variety of produce for summer sales: tomatoes, peppers, squash, potatoes and much more. We feature fresh picked produce when in season. We only use organic practices in our garden and on our farm. This means we do not use any GMO seeds, plants or materials, and of course no pesticides, herbicides, or any other fertilizers. We love eating straight from the garden, and we love sharing our food & techniques to show others organic is the way to go.  Thanks for supporting us!

Never underestimate the joy of growing your own Garden

Every year we discover new plants on our farm, some of them are gifts from the birds we've attracted.

Attracting Wildlife

Every year we see more & more wildlife on our farm. By growing gardens & leaving volunteer plants, we've discovered not only the beauty of attracting wildlife, but the benefits of having them. They create a natural pest control, they pollinate your plants & they are so beautiful to look at.

-- Our 3 Sister Planting--

Beans, corn & squash planted all in one to support each other. 

Beans naturally find support by climbing up the corn stalk.

Squash grows rapidly and the large squash leaves shade out weeds.

We collect seeds from our best plants.

When we don't have enough compost for the whole field, we start with where the seed goes first.

Wood chips work great for building up soil

We see amazing growth & strength when we feed our plants our compost tea.

We regularly add organic matter to our soil

We plant everywhere we can

We Love Mushrooms

You can grow a variety of gourmet mushrooms on logs and stumps, using mushroom plug spawn. Every year we collect all kinds of mushrooms around the farm, shiitakes, boletes, morels & more.
